Welcome to NC Transit Careers

WHY NOT A CAREER IN PUBLIC TRANSIT? The career opportunities in Public Transit in North Carolina are as diverse as the people and places of this beautiful state! From Call Center Support Specialists in Raleigh-Durham, to Mechanics in Wilmington…Enrollment and Eligibility Managers in Asheville to Vehicle Drivers in Charlotte…Accounting, Planning, Legal, and much more. Your career options have never looked brighter! There are a variety of specialized skill sets needed to make our dynamic Public Transit systems operate efficiently. Now's the time to look beyond the driver's seat.


BENEFITS

Competitive Salaries

Make a Difference - Serve Those Who Need Community Support The Most!
